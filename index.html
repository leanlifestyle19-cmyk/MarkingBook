Your data stays on your device

Student names, parents' numbers, home addresses, marks — they live in your browser, not on someone's server. For PDPA peace of mind, that's not a compromise. It's the point.

02

No login, no subscription

Nothing to remember, nothing billing you monthly for tools you use ten minutes a week. Bookmark the page. That's the whole setup.

03

Built for one tutor, not a franchise

No "organisation settings", no seats, no onboarding call. It speaks your week: assignments, packages, PayNow, parent chat, marks.

Made local

Quotes that know SG market rates so you don't overquote. Reschedule and reminder messages that sound like a Singaporean tutor, not a US SaaS.
